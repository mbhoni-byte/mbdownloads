Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

MBdownloads is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our video downloading service.

1. Information We Collect

When you use MBdownloads, we may collect the following information:

  • Video URLs: The links you submit for downloading
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device information, and IP address
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, download history, and interaction with our service
  • Payment Information: For premium subscriptions (processed securely by our payment partners)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Process your video download requests
  • Improve and optimize our service
  • Provide customer support
  • Process premium subscription payments
  • Detect and prevent technical issues or abuse

3. Data Storage and Retention

MBdownloads follows these data practices:

  • Video URLs are temporarily stored for processing and deleted after download completion
  • Downloaded videos are not stored on our servers
  • Analytics data is retained for 30 days
  • Premium account information is stored until account deletion

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences (like ad-free status for premium users)
  • Analyze service usage through Google Analytics
  • Display relevant advertisements (for free users)

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that collect information:

  • Payment Processors: Stripe and PayPal for premium subscriptions
  • Analytics: Google Analytics to understand usage patterns
  • Advertising: Google AdSense to display ads to free users

6. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmissions
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to personal data

However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

MBdownloads is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of data collection
  • Lodge complaints with data protection authorities

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes through our website or email (for premium users).
